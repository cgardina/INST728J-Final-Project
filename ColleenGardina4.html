Downtown Disney

Need a break from the parks? Take a break at Downtown Disney!

Dine


Downtown Disney boasts over 20 dining options, with enough variety to please any palate. Wake up with a coffee from Starbucks. Try a burger or famous CrazyShake at Black Tap Craft Burgers & Shakes or relax at the Uva Bar & Cafe. The best part is, you don't need a ticket to enter!



            Shop


Need a last minute souvenir? Downtown Disney has over 15 shops to browse. You can purchase Disney-themed home goods at Disney Home, Pandora jewelry, or even a lego set at the Lego Store.
